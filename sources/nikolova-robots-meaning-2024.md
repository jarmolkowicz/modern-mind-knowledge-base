# Nikolova et al. (2024) - Robots, Meaning, and Self-Determination

## Citation

Nikolova, M., Cnossen, F., & Nikolaev, B. (2024). Robots, meaning, and self-determination. *Research Policy*, 53(5), 104987.

**DOI:** [10.1016/j.respol.2024.104987](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.respol.2024.104987)

## Type

Paper (empirical)

## Key Insight

Automation reduces workers' sense of meaning and self-determination, even when job security is maintained. The psychological impact of automation goes beyond productivity to affect professional identity.
