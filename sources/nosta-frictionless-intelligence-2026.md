## Key Insight

AI inverts the cognitive constraints that make human meaning possible. Where human fluency follows understanding through struggle, AI fluency precedes comprehensionâ€”creating "confidence without consequence."

## Core Framework: Cognitive Inversion

| Human Cognition | AI Cognition |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Memory-bound | Stateless |
| Effortful understanding | Instant generation |
| Slow judgment through error/revision | Complete outputs appearing whole |
| Meaning requires metabolic cost | Confidence arrives costless |

## Key Quote

"Meaning, at least in the human sense, has never been free. It emerges through cost."
