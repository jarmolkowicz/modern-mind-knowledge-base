# Storm & Stone (2015) - Saving-Enhanced Memory

## Citation

Storm, B. C., & Stone, S. M. (2015). Saving-enhanced memory: The benefits of saving on the learning and remembering of new information. *Psychological Science*, 26(2), 182-188.

**DOI:** [10.1177/0956797614559285](https://doi.org/10.1177/0956797614559285)

## Type

Paper (experimental)

## Key Insight

When people save information externally (to a file), they remember it less well but free cognitive resources for learning new information. Demonstrates trade-off: offloading has benefits (freed capacity) and costs (reduced retention).
