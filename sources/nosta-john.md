# John Nosta

## Who

Innovation theorist and founder of NostaLab. Writes the "Digital Self" blog at Psychology Today, focusing on AI's impact on human cognition, identity, and meaning-making.

## Type

Thought leader (not empirical researcher)

## Relevance

Nosta's work directly addresses Modern Mind's core concerns: cognitive erosion through AI use, the value of productive struggle, and preserving human agency. His concepts provide fresh framings for established phenomena.

## Key Contributions

### Concepts Introduced

- **Cognitive Friction** - The effort required for thinking that gives it meaning; AI removes this friction
- **Borrowed Certainty** - Confidence in ideas we didn't earn through struggle
- **AI Oscillation Trap** - Performance degradation from switching between AI-assisted and manual work
- **Anti-Intelligence** - AI's capacity to relieve humans of the burden of thinking
- **Coherence Trap** - Mistaking fluent, coherent language for truth
- **Cognitive Entrapment** - Dependency disguised as partnership
- **Weightless Thinking** - Thought without consequence or commitment
